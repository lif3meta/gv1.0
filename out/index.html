QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is GivingVerse?

GivingVerse is a blockchain-based Profit With A Purpose platform and token designed to make the most social impact by allowing people to give towards charities and causes while earning rewards.

How we can buy and invest in the GivingVersetoken ?

The token will be available for pre-sale and public sale through all major DEXes and identified CEX.

Why we should choose the GivingVerse Token?

Just by buying an olding the token, you automatically start earning a proportioante percentage of the transaction fees for all transactions.

Which blockchain is the GivingVerse token available on?

The GivingVerse token is based on the Polygon network. Future development includes cross-chain bridging to other networks such as Fantom, Ethereum and Binance.

How secure is the token ?

The token contracts are based on the latest Open Zeppelin standards, have been audited and all changes are behind a 24 hour time-lock to ensure full transparency. We additionally use multi-sig for all changes, which means that no changes are approved until 2/3 signatures are received. The multi-sig is made up of every member of the GivingVerse team and in the future will include elected members of the community.

How do I earn rewards/yield?

Holding or staking of the token means that you automatically recieve rewards in the form of the GivingVerse token. Your yield will be dependent on the number of tokens you hold, the price of the token and whether you've selected to stake your tokens into an auto-compounding vault.
