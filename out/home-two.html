                  QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

                  FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

                  What is GivingVerse?

                  GivingVerse is a blockchain-based Impact Investment platform and token designed to make the most social impact by allowing people to contribute towards reforestation while earning rewards.

                  How we can buy and invest in the GivingVersetoken ?

                  The token will be available for sale directly via various Decentralized Exchanges.

                  Why we should choose the GivingVerse Token?

                  Just by buying an holding the token, you automatically start earning a proportionate percentage of the transaction fees for all transactions.

                  Which charities & organizations receive donations?

                  Donations will be used to place planting orders for real trees with Trees Of The Future. Additional treasury funds will also be used to disburse to charities as voted on by the community. The donations will be transaparent on the blockchain.

                  Which blockchain is the GivingVerse token available on?

                  The GivingVerse token is based on the Fantom network. Future development includes multi-chain bridging.

                  How secure is the token ?

                  The token contracts are based on the latest Open Zeppelin standards, have been audited and all changes are behind a 24 hour time-lock to ensure full transparency. Our founders are KYCed and we additionally use multi-sig for all changes, which means that no changes are approved until 2/3 signatures are received. The multi-sig is made up of every member of the GivingVerse team and in the future will include elected members of the community.

                  How do I earn rewards/yield?

                  Holding or staking of the token means that you automatically recieve a percentage of all transaction fees. Your yield will be dependent on the number of tokens you hold, the price of the token and whether you've selected to stake your tokens into an auto-compounding vault.
